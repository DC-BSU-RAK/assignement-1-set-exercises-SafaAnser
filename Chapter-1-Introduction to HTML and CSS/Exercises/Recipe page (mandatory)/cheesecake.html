Cheesecake recipe

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert typically made with a base of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs. It is often set on a crust made from crushed graham crackers or biscuit crumbs, combined with butter and sometimes sugar. The filling is smooth and dense, with a slightly tangy flavor from the cream cheese, and it can be flavored with vanilla, chocolate, fruit, or even coffee. Cheesecake can be baked or made as a no-bake version, with the baked variety often having a golden-brown top. It's a versatile dessert, commonly topped with fruit, caramel, chocolate ganache, or a variety of other sweet toppings. Cheesecake is beloved for its indulgent texture and rich, comforting taste.

  • Cream cheese
  • Granulated sugar

This is the complete list of ingredients for making a Classic Cheesecake!

Instructions

~ For the Crust:

  1. 1 ½ cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (or digestive biscuits, crushed)
  2. ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  3. 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted

~ For the Cheesecake Filling:
  1. 3 (8-ounce) packages (680g) cream cheese, softened
  2. 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  3. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  4. 3 large eggs
  5. 1 cup (240ml) sour cream (optional, for extra creaminess)
  6. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (optional, helps with structure)
  7. ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream (for a rich texture)

~ Optional Topping:
  1. Fresh fruit (e.g., berries like strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)
  2. Fruit compote or fruit sauce (e.g., strawberry or raspberry)
  3. Whipped cream for garnish
