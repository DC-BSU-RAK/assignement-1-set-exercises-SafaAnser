Characters

Here are the main characters from Charlie and the Chocolate Factory (2005):

  1. Johnny Depp as Willy Wonka - The eccentric, reclusive chocolate maker who owns the factory.

  2. Freddie Highmore as Charlie Bucket - The kind-hearted, humble boy who wins the last golden ticket.

  3. David Kelly as Grandpa Joe - Charlie’s loving grandfather who accompanies him to the factory.

  4. Helena Bonham Carter as Mrs. Bucket - Charlie’s caring mother.

  5. Noah Taylor as Mr. Bucket - Charlie’s father.

  6. Missi Pyle as Mrs. Beauregarde - Violet Beauregarde's mother.

  7. Deep Roy as Oompa-Loompas - The factory workers who assist Willy Wonka. Deep Roy plays all the Oompa-Loompas through the use of digital effects.

  8. Julia Winter as Veruca Salt - The spoiled, bratty girl who demands everything she wants.

  9. Philip Wiegratz as Augustus Gloop - The gluttonous boy who loves chocolate.

  10. AnnaSophia Robb as Violet Beauregarde - The gum-obsessed girl who competes to be the best.

  11. Jordan Fry as Mike Teavee - The boy obsessed with television and technology.

  12. Christopher Lee as Dr. Wilbur Wonka - Willy Wonka's father, a dentist who forbids his son from eating candy.

    13. These are the main and supporting roles, but there are also various other minor characters and extras in the film.
